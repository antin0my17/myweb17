MLB Trade Simulation & Trade Tracker

TradeNinja is a web platform designed for baseball fans and amateur GMs who want to analyze, simulate, and discuss player trades like pros.

  • Learn More

Main Features

Virtual Trade Simulator

Simulate hypothetical trades between MLB teams and analyze their feasibility. Ideal for fans exploring creative trade ideas or analysts testing realistic scenarios.

MLB Trade Tracker

Track real-life MLB trades with contextual details and team implications. Ideal for staying updated or analyzing market trends.

Share & Rate Virtual Trades

Share your virtual or real-life trades and get feedback from others. Ideal for community-driven analysis and constructive discussion.

Player DB & Team Needs Analysis

Browse a database of MLB players with key stats and track each team’s positional needs — essential for trade planning and roster analysis.
